GST Slabs in India

  • 0% — Essential goods, unbranded food items
  • 5% — Basic necessities, household goods
  • 12% — Processed foods, business class air travel
  • 18% — Most services, electronics, most goods
  • 28% — Luxury goods, tobacco, automobiles
